How do I make more money?

WhatsBusy uses data science to constantly audit your data, notifying you of opportunities to make more money with plain English recommendations. Each recommendation comes with the expected financial gain, so you know what it’s worth before you do it. WhatsBusy then analyzes the results of the recommendations, proving its ROI.

Traditional Process

Hire Analyst
Analyst runs report
Analyst attempts to quantify the ROI of solving the problem
Analyst take action
Analyst attempts to identify the problem
Analyst attempts to synthesize a plan
Analyst runs another report to determine results

Data Science and Prescription

Recommendation is generated, complete with expected ROI
Business takes action
Result is analyzed automatically, revealing true ROI of action
